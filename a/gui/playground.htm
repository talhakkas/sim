HTML5 Video Voting

Vote on your favorite moments with HTML5 Video

HTML5 Video Voting allows you to vote for your favorite segments of a video by pressing the space bar while the video is playing. You can then jump to the most popular segments of the video by navigating the timeline or clicking on the bars in the graph.
